BIS Certification Notification for Mobile Devices

Published on July 5, 2025 | Category: BIS Certification

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) has released an official notification introducing updated requirements for mobile device certification under the CRS Scheme. Effective from September 1st, 2025, all mobile phones manufactured or imported into India must comply with the revised BIS CRS regulations.

This move aims to improve consumer safety, enhance product quality, and strengthen compliance across the electronics sector. Manufacturers are advised to prepare in advance and ensure that all relevant models are registered and tested according to the new norms.
